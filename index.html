Welcome To Seeta Ram Ji Ki Rasoi Bhookh Mite, Bhakti Bane

Inspired by the maryada (righteousness), compassion, and sacrifice of Bhagwan Shree Ram and Maa Seeta, Seeta Ram Ji Ki Rasoi is a sacred initiative dedicated to serving free, wholesome, and dignified meals to those in need. More than just a kitchen, it is a spiritual movement of seva (selfless service), deeply rooted in bhakti (devotion). Every meal served is a humble offering of love, respect, and care—honoring the divine values embodied by Seeta Ram.
