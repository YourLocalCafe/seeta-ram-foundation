Our Mission Serving With Devotion

To establish spiritually driven kitchens that serve saatvik bhojan (pure food) to hungry people with respect, purity, and devotion, in an atmosphere filled with Ram bhakti and seva bhavna.

Our Objectives Making A Difference

Daily Meals

Provide two meals daily (Lunch and Dinner) to needy individuals.

Empowerment

Empower local women and youth through seva-based employment.

Cultural Values

Promote Indian sanskar, spirituality, and cleanliness through food service.

Expansion

Build a chain of Rasoi centers that reflect compassion and unity.
